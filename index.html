01 — Problem & solution
Two years of waiting.
We fixed that.
Pharmaceutical stability testing is a regulatory requirement — but the timelines are broken. StabilityAI makes early extrapolation reliable and defensible.
v1.0 shipped · May 2026 Python · scikit-learn ICH Q1A compliant Zeneth · in silico Arrhenius kinetics
The problem

Conventional pharmaceutical stability testing under ICH Q1A requires real-time studies at 25°C that take 18–24 months before data is sufficient for regulatory submission. This delays drug approvals, costs resources, and slows patient access to medicines. Teams lack validated computational tools to bridge the gap.

The solution

StabilityAI ingests 6 months of impurity data, trains a per-temperature linear regression model, extrapolates impurity growth to 22 months using zero-order kinetics, calculates activation energy via the Arrhenius equation, and cross-validates with Zeneth in silico pathway scoring — all in minutes, with <1% error against real experimental data.

07 — Technology stack
Built with
Four core tools, each chosen deliberately for a specific role in the pipeline.
🐍
Python — scikit-learn, pandas, matplotlib
Core AI engine. LinearRegression model trained per temperature condition. pandas handles data ingestion and preprocessing. matplotlib generates the output degradation graph.
🧬
Zeneth — Lhasa Limited
In silico degradation pathway prediction. Used to cross-validate AI predictions against known chemical reaction mechanisms at pH 1–14 and 25–100°C.
📊
Microsoft Excel
Input data format and baseline extrapolation calculations. Used to verify AI model outputs and provide the structured dataset for model training.
📋
ICH Q1A(R2) guidelines
Regulatory framework governing all stability testing conditions, storage requirements, and data interpretation. Ensures all outputs are positioned within accepted pharmaceutical standards.
